Health Companion

This Calorie Calculator estimates your daily calorie requirement (TDEE) using your body data and activity level. It helps you manage weight, fitness and health goals effectively.

What is TDEE?

TDEE = Total Daily Energy Expenditure (calories your body burns in a day).

Why Activity Matters?

Higher activity means your calorie requirement increases significantly.

Weight Goals

Calorie deficit = weight loss, surplus = weight gain, balanced = maintenance.

Health Tip

Combine a balanced diet with regular exercise for better heart & metabolism health.
