Stroke Information

What is a Stroke?

A stroke occurs when blood flow to part of the brain is interrupted or reduced, preventing brain tissue from receiving oxygen and nutrients. Immediate medical attention is critical to reduce damage.

Types of Stroke

  • Ischemic Stroke: Caused by blocked blood vessels.
  • Transient Ischemic Attack (TIA): Temporary blockage.
  • Hemorrhagic Stroke: Caused by ruptured vessels.

Medical Risk Factors

  • High blood pressure
  • Heart disease
  • Diabetes
  • High cholesterol
  • Obesity

Behavioral Risk Factors

  • Smoking
  • Alcohol consumption
  • Lack of physical activity

Non-Modifiable Factors

  • Age
  • Family history
  • Gender
