Health Companion

The BMI Calculator helps you understand whether your current weight is healthy for your height. This is a fast, simple and important health indicator.

What BMI Tells You

BMI estimates body fat based on height and weight and helps check your general fitness range.

Why It Matters

A healthy BMI can reduce risk of diabetes, stroke, heart disease, and obesity-related complications.

Quick Tip

For best accuracy, enter your latest weight and correct height in cm.

Track Progress

Use this regularly and view your BMI record inside your Health Reports.
